Introducing Minecraft Overviewer
Minecraft Overviewer is Google Maps for Minecraft! A plugin created by a team of volunteer developers, it's able to fetch the Minecraft world and then render it in thousands of tiles. The tiles are saved and then placed together on a website like a puzzle which you can then view, zoom, and explore the world with.

It does not end there though! Everynight, the tiles of which alterations occured within, are re-rendered, and then used to replace the old tile on the website. This creates an expansive interactive experience that keeps you up-to-date about the current state of the world in near real-time!
