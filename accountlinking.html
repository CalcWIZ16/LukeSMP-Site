The best of both worlds

Link your Java account with your Bedrock account to play anywhere,
whether that be at your computer, or on the go!

About Linking

When you link your Java and Bedrock accounts, you unlock complete parity between playing on your computer and playing on your mobile device. By doing this, all your player data is stored as one file, on the server, meaning anything you do on Java Edition, is reflected on Bedrock Edition (and vice versa). This allows you to pick up on a mobile device or console, where you left off on your desktop!

GeyserMC

GeyserMC is what makes this all possible, in fact any Bedrock x Java server is probably powered by GeyserMC. It is an open-source collaboration project with CubeCraft Games. Their goal is to "allow Minecraft: Bedrock Edition users to join Minecraft: Java Edition servers as seamlessly as possible to allow for true cross-platform."

They are doing some amazing work in helping the Minecraft community come together. Thank you GeyserMC!
