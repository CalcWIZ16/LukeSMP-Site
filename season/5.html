World Generation and Spawn
When you join LukeSMP Season 5 you are immediately integrated within the ever-evolving storyline and history of the server. Look around and you'll soon realize that you are surrounded by 4 pillars that extend upwards into the sky, stranded on a piece of land suspended up above a lake central to the spawn region mountain range.

The world was generated in Minecraft version 1.20: The Tales and Trails update. This addition to the game introduces various features that are prominent right when you join such as the cherry blossom biomes and waiting to be explored, new structures known as "Trail Ruins". New items and blocks in this update include, but are not limited to, the new cherry and bamboo wood types, hanging signs, chisled bookshelves, armor trims, and ancient plants. And don't forget about the new mobs as well, the Sniffer and Camel.
