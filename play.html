Minecraft on Consoles
On the Xbox, Playstation, and Nintendo Switch editions of Minecraft, you are not able to add custom servers for security reasons. Due to this, you may not think that you're able to play on the LukeSMP but fear not, there's a workaround.

By using Bedrock Connect, a DNS server hosted by the community, you're able to connect and play on Minecraft servers not on Mojang's predetermined list. Select which platform you play on below for a full tutorial on how to set it up.
